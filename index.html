What is Trezor Bridge?

Trezor Bridge is a software solution designed by Trezor to enable communication between your hardware wallet and computer, replacing older browser extensions. Unlike plug-ins that browsers might block or that can become obsolete, Trezor Bridge is a lightweight program that runs independently, ensuring you can connect your wallet and manage your crypto assets with complete security. By downloading Trezor Bridge (Version 24.9.2) from the official Trezor site, you gain an up-to-date, stable, and secure experience.
